Palm Springs

Directed by Max Barbakow, this romantic comedy follows two wedding guests, played by Andy Samberg and Cristin Milioti, who find themselves stuck in a time loop, reliving the same day over and over again. As they navigate the peculiar situation, a unique romance unfolds.

To All the Boys: Always and Forever

The third installment in the "To All the Boys" trilogy, directed by Michael Fimognari. The film continues the romantic journey of Lara Jean Covey, played by Lana Condor, as she navigates the challenges of her senior year and plans for the future with Peter, played by Noah Centineo.

Chemical Hearts

Directed by Richard Tanne, "Chemical Hearts" is a teen romance drama that follows the evolving relationship between two high school students, played by Lili Reinhart and Austin Abrams, as they navigate the complexities of love, loss, and self-discovery.

The Half of It
