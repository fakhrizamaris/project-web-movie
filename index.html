Birdman or (The Unexpected Virtue of Ignorance)

Directed by Alejandro G. Iñárritu, "Birdman" is a black comedy that follows a washed-up actor (Michael Keaton) attempting to revive his career by staging a Broadway play. The film is notable for its innovative use of long takes.

Interstellar

Directed by Christopher Nolan, "Interstellar" is a visually stunning and emotionally charged science fiction film. The story follows a group of astronauts, including Matthew McConaughey and Anne Hathaway, as they embark on a space mission to save humanity from a dying Earth.

La La Land

Directed by Damien Chazelle, "La La Land" is a romantic musical that follows the story of a jazz musician (Ryan Gosling) and an aspiring actress (Emma Stone) as they navigate their careers and relationship in Los Angeles.

The Revenant

Directed by Alejandro G. Iñárritu, "The Revenant" is a survival drama that follows frontiersman Hugh Glass, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, as he seeks revenge against those who left him for dead in the harsh wilderness.

Gravity

Directed by Alfonso Cuarón, "Gravity" is a visually stunning and tense space thriller starring Sandra Bullock and George Clooney. The film follows two astronauts stranded in space after their shuttle is destroyed.
