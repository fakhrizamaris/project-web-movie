Inception

Directed by Christopher Nolan, "Inception" is a mind-bending science fiction thriller that explores the concept of dreams within dreams. Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, leads a team of skilled individuals skilled in the art of entering people's dreams to steal their deepest secrets.

The Shawshank Redemption

Based on a Stephen King novella, this classic film follows the life of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, as he navigates the harsh realities of prison life. Directed by Frank Darabont, "The Shawshank Redemption" is a tale of hope, friendship, and resilience.

The Dark Knight

Christopher Nolan's second entry in his Batman trilogy, "The Dark Knight," features Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger's iconic portrayal of the Joker. The film explores the thin line between heroism and villainy in the gritty city of Gotham.

Forrest Gump

Directed by Robert Zemeckis, "Forrest Gump" follows the life of a man with a low IQ, played by Tom Hanks. Despite his challenges, Forrest unwittingly becomes part of several defining moments in American history, all while pursuing his lifelong love, Jenny.
