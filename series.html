Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Directed by David Yates, this installment sees Harry facing skepticism about Voldemort's return. The Ministry of Magic refuses to acknowledge the threat, prompting Harry to form Dumbledore's Army. As tensions rise, a climactic battle occurs at the Ministry.

Also directed by David Yates, the sixth film delves into Voldemort's past as Harry learns about Horcruxes and the Half-Blood Prince. As the wizarding world teeters on the brink of war, relationships are tested, and a devastating tragedy occurs.
